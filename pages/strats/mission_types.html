Mission types

The first crucial step is to understand the three following mission types:

  • Burn em all mission
  • Run and burn
  • One and out
  • Fullpod mission

These missions involve a main objective requiring completion times which cannot be shortened. These moments are long enough to allow you to burn all reinforcements all along and kill yourself for the last time as soon as the "Mission completed" message appears on screen.

It is therefore to spot every and each of those incompressible moments in order to properly burn reinforcements as it shall not slow down main objective completion.

Each Burn em all mission has a dedicated page to be found on the left bar menu. They contain detailed explanations about death optimization.

Run and burn

A "Run and burn" mission allows you to burn part of the reinforcements while main objective is being completed. However, you will likely get it done before having all the reinforcements burnt.

It involves then a precise strategy to burn as many reinforcements as you can without slowing down main objective completion. Left reinforcements will be burned in rounds as every diver will kill themselves as they get out of their pod.

Each Run and burn mission has a dedicated page to be found on the left bar menu. They contain detailed explanations about death optimization.

These missions are amid the fastest ones to complete. On the other hand, they do not belong to the "Fullpod" mission category because you will need to call in a single stratagem to get main objective completed before going through the six rounds of death.

Unlike Run and burn mission, you won't have time to burn reinforcements while completing main objective.

These missions are the shortest ones. The strike force of your hellpod is enough to complete main objective. From its beginning to its end, the mission therefore consists in correctly guided hellpods and 6 rounds of frame perfect deaths.

Each Fullpod mission has a dedicated page to be found on the left bar menu. They contain detailed explanations about death optimization.

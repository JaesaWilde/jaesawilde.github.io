Overview

It is far the fastest high level mission to complete in Any%. A god seed will allow you to trigger the end in under 1:20. Even if you have to deal with Stratagem Jammers or unlucky outposts disposition, you should be able to destroy enough fabricators in under 1:40.

The key aspect at level 10 is: Fortress. In a multiplayer context, bear in mind that it does not contain enough fabricators to complete main objective. A common mistake is to pod within Fortress then realising you need more fabricators and loosing time finding them.

The strategy we recommend is to use Fortress and full squad death respawn mechanic at your advantage. When the full squad dies and Helldivers reinforce all at once, they are likely to repod around the position where the first of them died.

We therefore recommend to position the drop position pin slightly outside Fortress, having it surrounded by the most red areas of the map. The walkthrough part details what to do.

Walkthrough

  1. Each Helldiver picks a direction to pod in:
    • One in Fortress (must die first to make the whole squad reinforce into Fortress)
    • Others in a red area (north, south, east, west)
  2. The main stratagem you want to use is the Orbital Laser. In a god run, you want to throw it in a red area in your cardinal direction without walking then immediately kill yourself. Orbital Laser targets fabricators and can be thrown in range of an Anti-Air Emplacement
    • The Eagles stratagems ( Eagle 500kg Bomb, Eagle Airstrike, Eagle Strafing Run) may help you to destroy fabricators if you see a Factory Strider in the vicinity of your red area - as the Orbital Laser will target it instead of fabricators that are around
    • If you are in range of an Anti-Air Emplacement, you shall throw your Orbital Laser and/or use Thermite grenades before killing yourself
    • If you are in range of a Stratagem Jammer that you are unable to destroy (too far away, missed Ultimatum shot), run to the closest fabricators around you and use you Thermite grenades and/or your Ultimatum shot
  3. Perform full squad death rounds to trigger the end of the mission

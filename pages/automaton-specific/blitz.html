Overview

It is far the fastest high level mission to complete in Any%. A god seed will allow you to trigger the end in under 1:20. Even if you have to deal with Stratagem Jammers or unlucky outposts disposition, you should be able to destroy enough fabricators in under 1:40.

The key aspect at level 10 is: Fortress. In a multiplayer context, bear in mind that it does not contains enough fabricators to complete main objective. A common mistake is to pod within Fortress then realising you need more fabricators and loosing time finding them.

The strategy we recommend is to use Fortress and full squad death respawn mechanic at your advantage. When the full squad dies and Helldivers reinforce all at once, they are likely to repod around the position where the first of them died.

We therefore recommend to position the drop position pin slightly outside Fortress, having it surrounded by the most red areas of the map. The walkthrough part details what to do.

Walkthrough

  1. Each Helldiver picks a direction to pod in:
    • One in Fortress
    • Others in a red area (north, south, east, west)
  2. Each Helldivers throws the most appropriate stratagem, usually Orbital Laser as it targets fabricators
    • The Helldiver targeting Fortress MUST be the first to die. You will then all reinforce into it and be able to destroy its fabricators while burning reinforcements
    • If there is a Factory Strider in the vicinity of your target area, Eagles and Termites might be preferred
    • If you are in the range of a Stratagem Jammer either destroy it with the Ultimatum then use an Orbital Laser or use the Ultimatum along with Termites in order to take down at least one fabricator before killing yourself
  3. Perform frame-perfect deaths to trigger end mission

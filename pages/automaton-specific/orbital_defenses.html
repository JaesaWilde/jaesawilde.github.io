Overview

With the arrival of the Portable Hellbomb, this mission is now way more trivial it used to be in Any%. The strategy is pretty straightforward. Although only having three orbital cannons to take down, being four runners is a certain gain of time.

Walkthrough

  1. As soon as you exit your pod, call in the Jump Pack in the direction of your orbital cannon
  2. As soon as you get a good angle, throw the Portable Hellbomb stratagem into the outpost so it lands at the feet of the orbital cannon
  3. You may use the Orbital Laser and Eagle Strafing Run to make your way through. As soon as your Portable Hellbomb is activated, kill yourself
  4. Perform full squad death rounds to trigger the end of the mission

Overview

With the arrival of the Portable Hellbomb, this mission is now way more trivial it used to be in Any%. The strategy is pretty straightforward. Although only having three orbital cannons to take down, being four runners is a certain gain of time.

Walkthrough

  1. Designate three Helldivers to take down orbital cannons. The fourth one will kill themselves at least 4 times
  2. Throw the Portable Hellbomb stratagem as you approach the orbital cannon outpost. The ideal is to grab it as you keep running to the cannon
  3. As soon as you activate it, kill yourself
  4. Perform frame-perfect deaths to trigger end mission

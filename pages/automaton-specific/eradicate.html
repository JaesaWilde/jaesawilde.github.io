Overview

Along with Eradicate Terminid Swarm, this is the only mission with a strategy similar to that used in 100%. The aim is not to deplete reinforcements, but to proceed to extraction. The major difference is that in 100%, all Helldivers must extract, whereas in Any% the fastest way to go involves extracting a single runner and the death of all the others.

For this exact reason, wide bombardment stratagems such as Orbital 380mm HE Barrage must not be used, at the risk of killing the Helldiver who will be extracted.

A good reference time is 1:50. Below, the run is excellent. Around 2:00 the run is average.

Walkthrough

The key in Eradicate runs is not to use any stratagem before the Portable Hellbombs explode. The reason why is: Portable Hellbombs can multiply kills (as well as Seeker grenades) while other stratagems cannot. In a god run, using them is enough to complete main objective.

  1. Call in Portable Hellbombs and don't engage in combat. You want to let light Automatons to pop a flare and enemy clusters to bloom. Use your Seekers grenades only after getting the flare.
  2. Pick up a Portable Hellbomb and look for clusters of enemies. The best of the best is a Factory Strider giving birth to Devastators.
  3. As soon as your Portable Hellbomb is used and you've been reinforced, keep throwing Seeker grenades and other stratagems ( Eagle 500kg Bomb, Orbital Laser, Orbital Gas Strike) as long as it does not interfere with the blossom of a Portable Hellbomb-friendly cluster of enemies
  4. As Pelican lands, a Helldiver prepares to board while the others kill themselves to trigger the end of the mission.

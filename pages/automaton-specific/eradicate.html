Overview

A wonderful no-brainer mission. A great Any% one is a Burn em all. All the reinforcements are properly burned down to 0 throughout the mission without slowing down its completion. Otherwise, it will become a Run a burn one and it has to be avoided at all costs.

A good reference time is 2:00. Below, the run is excellent. Above 2:20, the run is average.

Walkthrough

  1. Designate a runner that will mostly kill themselves all along the run
  2. Just after you pod, call in the Portable Hellbombs and squad kill twice to let them and enemies spawn. Do it while letting light bots popping a flare to increase dropships number
  3. Three runners grab a Hellbomb while the fourth one kills themselves over and over
  4. Seek for a big clusters of enemies to throw yourself with a Hellbomb. The cherry on the top is a Factory Strider. You may also throw Seeker grenades at little groups of enemies in order to multiply kills
  5. When your Hellbomb is used, throw Seeker grenades as you respawn and kill yourself to burn reinforcements
  6. If the Hellbombs were not enough to complete main objective, throw everything you have. Lasers, Orbital Barrages, Eagles
    • Keep an eye on how many reinforcements are left in order to burn the last ones as you approach 100% completion without risking to trigger the end of the mission before it is completed

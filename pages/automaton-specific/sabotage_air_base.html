Overview

Well executed, this mission can be a speedrun delight as well as being a true death loop hell - especially in an un-scouted context.

The mission consists of destroying a Tower and two Dropships emplacements. To take down the Tower is the most dangerous part. As the strategy is to directly pod into it not to loose time climbing it, you will likely have to deal with heavy armored enemies and a drop bot.

Don't take Vitality Enhancement Booster. It will prevent the Tower Helldivers to one shot themselves with the Exploding Crossbow.

Walkthrough

  1. Each Helldiver picks a role. Two on Tower, two (or one) on Dropships. The following steps focus on the Tower Helldivers as Dropships Helldivers only mission is to throw an Orbital Laser/Eagle in order to destroy Dropships then immediately kill themselves
  2. Pod into Tower. One goes at the feet of the Tower and immediately calls in SSD. The other goes to the terminal. Throw Orbital Lasers and Orbital Railcannon Strike in order to clear Tower base
  3. Call Shield Generator Relay for terminal Helldiver. Once SSD Helldiver is done: kill yourself
  4. Terminal Helldiver reinforces the others until they trigger the 10s cooldown to make Tower explode
  5. Perform frame-perfect deaths to trigger end mission

Overview

One of the fastest missions to complete in Any%, along with Blitz: Search and Destroy. The usual way was to split from the most equidistant landing point, run to the bunker and throw an Orbital Laser on it. Although this strategy is still viable, a faster one focuses the use of the MLS-4X Commando stratagem.

You can also be even faster by using the FAF-14 Spear support weapon. However, the MLS-4X Commando is less fickle in terms of aiming. In a scouted context, FAF-14 Spear is preferable. In an un-scouted context, especially during an operation speedrun, MLS-4X Commando is preferable. In both cases, you have to aim for the same weak spot of the bunkers.

Bunkers are more or less square structures with a turret atop each corner. You want to aim for any point in the vertical continuity of one of these top turrets. With the FAF-14 Spear, it will be a one-shot deal. With the MLS-4X Commando, you'll have to fire four times. The most obvious advantage is the ability to fire from a great distance - the MLS-4X Commando absence of bullet drop and auto-aim gives it a huge range.

Unlike orbital cannons which can be seen and heard from quite a distance, bunkers are harder to spot in an un-scouted operation with the Poor Intel - Objective locations are hidden modifier.

To spot your bunker as soon as you land, you'll need a Scout armour. Scan the red areas with your pin for a shape like the one shown below. It's a bigger, squarer dot than the fabricators/Scout Strider/Hulk/Tanks/Cannon Turret. When you zoom in to the maximum, the shape is much more distinguishable.

Walkthrough

  1. One Helldiver goes after a bunker
  2. Call in FAF-14 Spear/MLS-4X Commando as soon as you get a good angle
  3. Aim for the weak spot
  4. Perform frame-perfect deaths to trigger end mission

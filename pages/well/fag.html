Any% basics · French Autistic Glossary

French English equivalent Usage context Slow pronunciation Normal pronunciation
Salut Hi / goodbye Informal way of saluting someone / saying goodbye at any time of day
Bonjour Hello Common way of saluting someone in daylight
Bonsoir Hello Common way of saluting someone during the evening / night
Comment ça va ? How are you doing? Informal way of asking how someone is. Works for one or more person(s)
Comment vas-tu ? How are you doing? Informal way of asking how someone is. Works for a single person
Comment allez-vous ? How are you doing? Common way of asking how someone is. Works either for at least two people or a single person you want to adress with a mark of respect
Ça va, merci I'm doing good, thanks Common way of saying you're doing well
Tranquille I'm chilling Informal way to say you're doing well
Pépouze I'm chilling Slang way of saying you're doing well. Oral deformation of the slang words "pépère" meaning "chilling"
Réanimation Reinforce me To signal that you need reinforcement right now. Can be shortened to the first syllable "réa".
Rés Reinforce me Shorter way to signal that you need reinforcement right now
Joli tir Nice shot Congratulating someone on a shot they just delivered
Bien joué Nice one / well played Congratulating someone on any great in-game action
J'y suis presque Almost there / I'm almost done Way to say either you're almost done doing something or you've almost got somewhere
C'est bon Done / it's alright Common way to say either you're done doing something or you control the situation
Allez, allez come on, come on / move on Express your hurry to get things done or urging someone to do something / go somewhere
T'es sérieux ? Seriously? / Are you kidding me? Express your dissatisfaction with the way things are going. Works either by addressing someone or by talking impersonally about the game
Tu te fous de ma gueule ? Seriously? / Are you fucking kidding me? Express your dissatisfaction with the way things are going in a more crude way. Works either by addressing someone or by talking impersonally about the game
Je vais crever I am going to die Signal that you are about to die an unfortunate death
Putain Fuck The most appropriate French word for any situation. Excitement, joy, anger, relief, sadness, despair, etc.
Fait chier Shit A very French expression to express irritation
Merde Shit A very French expression to express irritation
Enculé Son of a bitch/motherfucker A very French expression to express irritation
Bordel For fuck's sake A very French expression to express irritation
J'ai merdé I fucked up A very French way of saying you've screwed up
J'ai chié I fucked up A very French way of saying you've screwed up. Also works to say you've had a shit
J'ai chié dans la colle I fucked up A very French way of saying you've screwed up. Literally means "I crapped in the glue"
J'ai fait n'importe quoi I fucked up Least vulgar way of saying you've screwed up. Can be shortened to "j'ai fait n'imp"

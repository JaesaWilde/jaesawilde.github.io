Cardinal direction

To speed up the execution of an operation, ensure that each runner is assigned a cardinal direction and/or a specific role for certain missions. If there is no main objective in the primary direction, the secondary direction should be referred to. For instance:

Runner Primary direction Secondary direction
J1 West South
H2 South West
L3 North East
D4 East North
Runner Role (for Sabotage Air Base)
J1 Tower (terminal)
H2 Dropships (South or West)
L3 Tower (SSD)
D4 Dropships (North or East)

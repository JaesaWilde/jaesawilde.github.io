Who are we?

Four Frenchmen who decided, on May 2024, to officially create the F.A.S: an Any% speedrun squad on Helldivers II. F.A.S stands for French Autistic Squad. We quickly established ourselves on the Any% leaderboards on speedrun.com. This website brings together most of our knowledge about this specific category.

About other categories

Helldivers II speedrun revolves around three categories:

  • Any% · complete main objective then end the mission
  • Extract% · complete main objective then proceed to extraction
  • 100% · complete everything (main objective, optional objectives, outposts) then proceed to extraction
